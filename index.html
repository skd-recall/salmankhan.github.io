03 / Final Year Project
Final Year Project · In Progress

AI Gym Trainer

A mobile fitness app that gives users personalized workout and diet plans, tracks progress, and corrects exercise form in real time — with an AI chatbot on hand for guidance along the way.

ProblemMost home workouts have no one watching your form — small mistakes in posture go uncorrected and turn into injuries over time.
How it worksCamera feed → pose landmarks → joint-angle calculation → rule-based form scoring → instant on-screen feedback, live while you train.
ArchitectureClient-server today: mobile frontend + FastAPI backend + MongoDB. The posture engine is being rebuilt as a standalone React Native module running a TFLite pose model fully on-device, so form correction works offline with zero network round-trip.

02 / Deep Learning

Housing Price Prediction

Predicting house prices from more than just spreadsheet numbers.

ChallengeListing photos carry real pricing signal that tabular features alone miss — but images and numbers don't fuse easily.
ApproachFused a ResNet18 CNN over listing images with engineered tabular features in one multimodal model, tuned and evaluated on MAE/RMSE.
Result92% accuracy — a 13% lift over the tabular-only baseline.

03 / ML Pipeline

Titanic Survival Prediction

The classic ML problem, built the way production pipelines actually should be.

ChallengeMessy, partly-missing passenger data that needed to generalize cleanly to unseen records, not just fit a notebook.
ApproachScikit-learn Pipeline + ColumnTransformer for imputation, scaling and one-hot encoding, SelectKBest for feature selection, and GridSearchCV to tune a Random Forest.
Result+10% performance from feature selection alone; final pipeline exported and reusable via pickle.
